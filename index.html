side eye is a design studio with a bias for the unconventional

side eye stands for a critical glance, a viewpoint of a new generation with the motto "to see and be seen." side eye is here to inspire, ask questions, play with conventions and norms, and break them. side eye is curious and brave, young yet timeless, living in the present and future. side eye wants to change things while having fun. Seriousness when needed. The offer is original, trendy, yet lasting solutions in a world of change and transformation.

our special services

side eye stands for a holistic view of design while strategy development is an integral part of the service. Brands are developed, identity and positioning are in focus. We develop visual landscapes, for digital as well as analog media. Whether it comes to branding, print media, motion design or social media - the channel follows the purpose.

meet our team

side eye is a six-person team of dedicated students offering a variety of skills: From talented copywriters to graphic designers and motion designers, a broad toolset of skills is offered. side eye believes that the present needs young, unconventional and sustainable solutions. We love what we do and enjoy aiming for and creating something new. Our eyes are sharpened on the future, the only open question: Are you ready to take the first step with us?
